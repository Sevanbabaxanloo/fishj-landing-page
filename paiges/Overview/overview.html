    Overview

    Carp France at Jonchery

    Jonchery is a stunning 30 acre lake set in the heart of the Champagne region, created and run by the prominent fish farming Bachelier family. This lake has a great reputation, stretching back many years, for offering superb carp fishing holidays.


    The 6 large double swims give you loads of options on where and how to fish - you'll never be short of water. Action is usually prolific too thanks to its stock of around 360 carp, with an excellent head at 40lb+ and a lake record at over 70lb.


    You pre-book swims individually or the lake exclusive for up to 12 anglers. All swims must be booked by two anglers, with the exception of swims 1 and 5 which can be booked by a single angler if required.


    The lake also offer optional food packages and bait on site.


    Around 100m away is sited the smaller sister lakes Jonchery 7 & Jonchery 8 which you can book alongside this lake to accept groups of up to 18 anglers on the complex.
